Headquartered in Mumbai, Bank of India is a financial services provider commercial bank founded in 1906. It is a type of a public company providing retail as well as commercial banking facility to the consumers with a total of above 4545 bank-branches controlled through 50 Zonal offices.


Our Values

Our Services

Star Home Loan

Provides home loans up to Rs.500 lakh where repayment ranges up to 30 years, with reasonable margin and nominal processing charges. BOI star home loan offers free personal accident insurance cover to its customers.

Star Personal Loan

Provides Personal loans up to Rs.500 lakh where repayment ranges up to 30 years, with reasonable margin and nominal processing charges. BOI star home loan offers free personal accident insurance cover to its customers.

Credit Card
